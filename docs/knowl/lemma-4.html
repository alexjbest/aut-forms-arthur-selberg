Lemma1.81

Let \(T \gt 1\) and \(x+ iy\) in the standard fundamental domain \(\mathcal F\) for \(\SL_2(\ZZ) \backslash \HH\) then

\begin{equation*} E_T(z; s) = \begin{cases} E(z; s) \amp y\lt T\\ E(z; s) - \pi^{-s} \Gamma(s) \zeta(2s) y^s \amp y\ge T \end{cases}\text{.} \end{equation*}
