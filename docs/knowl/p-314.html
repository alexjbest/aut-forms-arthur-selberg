TFAE

  1. \begin{equation*} \{x_\lambda\}_{\lambda \in L} \text{ is }\mu \text{ equidistributed on } \Omega \end{equation*}
  2. For all polynomials \(P\) with \(\RR\) coefficients
    \begin{equation} \frac{\trace(P(H_\lambda))}{d_\lambda} \to_{\lambda \to \infty} \pair{P}{\mu}\tag{2.5} \end{equation}
  3. For all \(m \in \ZZ_{\ge 0}\) there exists a polynomial \(P\) of degree \(m\) s.t. (2.5) is satisfied.
