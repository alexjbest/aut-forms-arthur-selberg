We have \(K \hookrightarrow \adeles_K\text{.}\)

  1. \(K\) is discrete in \(\adeles_K\text{.}\)
  2. \(K\) is cocompact in \(\adeles_K\) (i.e. \(K \backslash \adeles_K\) is compact).
  3. \begin{equation*} K \cap \adeles_{K,\infty} = K \cap \prod_{v|\infty} K_v = \ints _K\text{.} \end{equation*}
  4. \(K + \adeles_{K,\infty} = \adeles_K\text{.}\)
  5. \(\mu(K\backslash \adeles_K) = 1\) (with our earlier conventions).
  6. \(K^\perp = K\) i.e.
    \begin{equation*} \{\alpha \in \adeles_K : e(\Lambda(\alpha \eta) ) = 1 \,\forall \eta \in K \} = K\text{.} \end{equation*}
