Theorem 1.65

Let \(\phi(x+iy) = O(y^{-N})\) for all \(N\gt 0\) and \(\phi(z)\) is invariant under \(z\mapsto \gamma z\) for \(\gamma\in \SL_2(\ZZ)\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \int_{\SL_2(\ZZ)\backslash \HH} \phi(z) E(z; s) \diff z \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = \pi^{-s} \Gamma(S) \zeta(2s) \tilde a_0 (s-1)\text{.} \end{equation*}
